About us

It is with great pleasure that we introduce to you “Al Shad Clinics” located at Street near to the Jaser Baipass,Narowal. We thank Almighty Allah & all our satisfied patients who have encouraged us to establish this new Centre.

Al Shad Clinics, is a specially designed facility to provide healthcare services with a high level of expertise in a very professional atmosphere.

We are offering our sincere services for patients through a multitude of highly qualified and well experienced medical specialists along with supporting medical and paramedical staff from different parts of South Asian countries. It will be our pleasure to serve you.

Our entire team, from the physicians and nurses to all the staff who support them, has one goal – to provide the highest quality care & service. We are dedicated to offering you and your loved ones excellent healthcare service at affordable prices.

We wish you the best of health always and we assure you that we will be there when you need us to provide you with an unmatched level of healthcare.
